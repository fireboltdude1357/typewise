## Pokémon Showdown and `@pkmn`

Typewise uses the `@pkmn/data`, `@pkmn/dex`, `@pkmn/img`, `@pkmn/mods`, and
`@pkmn/sim` packages and displays sprites served by Pokémon Showdown. The
checked-in `src/data/core-game-data.json` file is a transformed legality and
title-roster supplement generated from these packages and a pinned Pokémon
Showdown source snapshot.
